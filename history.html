History

Learn about the history of Bay City

Bay City was created on March 3, 2018, by Jerry Zhang. From 2018 to 2019, Bay city was small town on the coastline of The Bay. During 2019, Bay City was able to develop at an amazing speed.

In 2020, the great Bay Area was formed with the plan of the Bay Area Perimeter expressway and the city of North Bay. In 2021, the city continues to develop at a high speed towards the goal of a green city in 2022.
