History

Learn about the history of Bay City

Bay City was created on March 3, 2018 by Jerry Zhang. From 2018 - 2019, Bay city was built on mainly what is called Rockville now. During 2019, with the addition of structure blocks to Minecraft, Bay City was able to develop skyscrapers at an amazing speed

In 2020, the great Bay Area was formed with the plan of the Bay Area Perimeter expressway and the city of North Bay
